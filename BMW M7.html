BMW M7

The BMW M7 CS is a high-performance flagship sedan that combines the luxury of the 7 Series with the sporty dynamics of BMW M, featuring up to 660 hp, all-wheel drive, and advanced M Performance technologies.

    Key Features & Specs (Current Generation)

    M Sport steering wheel with paddle shifters. M high-gloss brake calipers and aerodynamic wheels. Exhaust system with multiple electronically controlled flaps for adaptive sound. Exclusive M Performance packages and M-specific drive programs.
