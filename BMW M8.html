BMW M8

The BMW M8 Competition Coupe is a high-performance luxury coupe featuring a 625 hp twin-turbo V8 engine, M xDrive all-wheel-drive system, adaptive M suspension, and a host of advanced interior, exterior, and technology features for ultimate driving experience.

    Key Features & Specs (Current Generation)

    Performance and Engine Engine: 4,395 cc M TwinPower Turbo V8 producing 625 hp at 6,000 rpm and 750 Nm torque from 1,800–5,600 rpm. Transmission: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic with manual mode and gearshift paddles, combined with M xDrive AWD, a selectable 2WD mode, and an M Sport Differential for enhanced traction. Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in 3.2–3.45 seconds depending on the variant. Suspension: Adaptive M suspension with Comfort, Sport, and Sport Plus modes, featuring double-wishbone front and five-link rear axles. M Sport Brakes: High-performance braking system with optional M Carbon ceramic brakes and red or matte gold calipers for superior stopping power.
