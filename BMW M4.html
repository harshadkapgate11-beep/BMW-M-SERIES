BMW M4

The BMW M4 CS is a high-performance sports coupe combining racetrack capabilities with luxury, featuring a 3.0-litre twin-turbo inline-six engine producing 543 bhp and 650 Nm torque, accelerating 0-100 km/h in 3.4 seconds, and priced from ₹1.89 crore in India.

    Key Features & Specs (Current Generation)

    0–100 km/h in 3.6 seconds 503–543 hp inline-six turbo engine 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission xDrive AWD with rear-biased and rear-wheel-drive modes Adaptive M suspension with multiple driving modes Lightweight CFRP components for performance and aesthetics Premium interior with Alcantara, Merino leather, and advanced infotainment Advanced safety and driver assistance systems.
